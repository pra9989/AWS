Natural Language Processing(NLP)
NLP bridges the gap b/w human communication and computer understanding 
Core NLP tasks:
Understanding
Generation
Interaction
Applications of NLP:
> Chatbots
> Machine transalation
> Sentiment Analysis
> Text Summarization
Prompts:
 A Prompt is an input given to a language model to instruct it on what to do
 prompts can be simple instructions questions or even complete paragraphs

Foundation Models:
Foundation models are large-scale language models trained on massive datasets of text and code
Examples: GPT, BERT, LaMDA
Key Characteristics of Foundation Models 
Versatility: Adapatable to various NLP tasks with minimal fine-tunning.
Knowledge: Capture externsive knowledge about the world from their training data.
Efficiency: Reduce the need for training seperate models for each task

Large Language Models(LLMs):
LLms are the type of foundation model characterized by their massive size(billions or trillons of parameters)
This scale allows them to generate remarkably human like text and exhibit impressive language understanding capabilities.
Key Capabilities:
 Text Generation: Writing stories, poems, articles and code.
 Translation: Acuurately translating b/w multiple languages.
 Question answering: Providing comprehensive and informative answers
 Dialogue: Engaging in natural and coherent conversations
