LifeCycle of Foundation Models
Introduction to Foundation Models 
Foundation models are massive AI modeles trained on a vast amount of data capable of adapting to a wide range of tasks
Characterized by its massive scale with billions of parameters adaptable for diverse applications and capable of transferring knowledge b/w tasks
Examples: GPT-4o for language and DALL-E for image generation, BERT for natural language understanding
Revolutionizing AI by providing a foundation for building more specialized and efficient models.
Stage1: Pre-training 
Data: Foundation models are trained on massive datasets of text images code or other data
objective: learn general representations and patterns in the data
Process: The model is exposed to the data and adjusts its parameters to predict the next word pixel or code token.
Outcome: A model with a broad understanding of the data
Analogy: Like learningthe grammar and vocabulary
